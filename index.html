Track the things you care about

Remove the distractions and find more time for yourself

Borrowing ideas from Essentialism, our app will help you organize and reflect on your most important values and declutter your mind, so that you can stay focused on the things that bring meaning to your life.

Daily journaling and value reminders can help you focus on the big picture. Keep track of your thoughts, commitments, projects, and experiences.

How does it work?

1

Select up to 3 things that you value most

2

Reflect on those values with a short entry

3

Add projects or journal entries to each of your values. Relate the things you love to your values.

4

Return to the app as often as you like, or set up reminders to help you maintain your practice.
